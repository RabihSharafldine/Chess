Grandmasters - Candidates

Hikaru Nakamura

American chess grandmaster, youtuber and streamer.

Grandmaster at age 15, five time USA chess champion and the World Fischer Random Chess Champion in 2022.

Fabiano Caruana

Italian and American chess grandmaster at age 14.

Third highest rated player of all time with a peak rating of 2844 and USA chess champion three times.

Ian Nepomniachtchi

Russian chess grandmaster and former World Chess Champion challenger.

Grandmaster at age 17, two time Chess Championship Challenger in 2021 and 2023.

Dommaraju Gukesh

Indian chess grandmaster and third youngest Grandmaster in history.

The youngest winner of the FIDE Candidates tournament. Gukesh won the 2024 Candidates Tournament, making him the youngest contender to compete for the title of World Chess Champion.

Alireza Firouzja

Iranian and French chess grandmaster at age 14.

Firouzja won the Iranian Chess Championship at age 12, and the FIDE Grand Swiss tournament winner in 2021.

Praggnanandhaa

Indian chess grandmaster at age 13.

He achieved his first grandmaster norm at the World Junior Chess Championship in November 2017, and achieved the second place in wthe Chess World Cup in 2023 against Magnus Carlsen

Vidit Gujrathi

Indian chess grandmaster at age 19.

Vidit won the FIDE Grand Swiss tournament in 2023, and winner of the Tata Steel Challengers in 2018.

Nijat Abasov

Azerbaijani chess grandmaster at age 16.

In October 2023, he reached his all time highest rating of 2679 and was ranked number 58 in the world, and he participate in the candidates 2024 due to Magnus resignation from the tournament.
